The single source of truth for the List With Ralph brand. Across the website, flyers, the Seller Portal, and every printed piece, this is what keeps the look consistent: a calm luxury palette built on the 60-30-10 rule, two classic typefaces, and a single gold accent that signals quality. When something looks off, match it back to this page.

01The 60-30-10 rule

Every layout follows one proportion. Cream carries most of the space and keeps everything light and confident. Charcoal grounds the design and carries the words. Gold appears last and least, reserved for the moments that should feel special. Keep gold scarce and it stays luxurious.

60Cream · Dominant
30Charcoal · Secondary
10Gold · Accent

60 Cream: page and section backgrounds, cards, breathing room.

30 Charcoal: dark sections, body text, primary buttons, photography.

10 Gold: eyebrows, hairline rules, the signature mark, calls to action.

04The signature mark

A four-point gold star sits at the heart of the brand. It marks eyebrows, list items, success states, and quiet dividers. Use it small and in gold. It is punctuation, not a logo, so it never competes with the wordmark.

06Voice & tone

Confident, warm, and plain-spoken. Ralph sounds like a trusted expert who tells it straight: no hype, no jargon, no pressure. Lead with the seller's benefit, back it with what actually gets done, and always keep things transparent.

  • Client first. Speak to the seller's goals, timeline, and peace of mind before features.
  • Transparent. Plain language about what happens and what comes next. Never leave them guessing.
  • Confident, not loud. Results speak. Avoid exclamation marks and salesy superlatives.
  • Warm and human. Write the way Ralph talks, like a knowledgeable friend in the business.

The em dash rule

Never use em dashes anywhere in Ralph's content. Use commas, colons, periods, or parentheses instead. This applies to the website, flyers, documents, and social posts.

07Design tokens

Drop these custom properties into the site stylesheet and reference them everywhere. One source for every value above, so a brand change propagates in a single edit.

/* Ralph Richardson brand tokens */
:root {
  /* 60 dominant */

  /* 30 secondary */
  --ink:      #1A1A1A;  --ink-2:   #23211E;  --ink-soft: #3A3631;
  /* 10 accent */

}
