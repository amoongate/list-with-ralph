About this property

Single-story home with a designer kitchen.

Showcasing over $90,000 in high-end upgrades, this four-bedroom, single-story residence has been thoughtfully reimagined with refined materials and elevated finishes. The result is a sophisticated, move-in-ready home in one of Riverside's most desirable communities.

Designer kitchen

The kitchen is a true showpiece, anchored by a professional-grade Thermador appliance suite with double convection ovens, an electric cooktop, a custom hood, and a built-in microwave drawer. Calcutta honed marble flows across the countertops, oversized island, and full-height backsplash. Custom soft-close cabinetry in Sherwin-Williams Solstice pairs with a warm white oak island, Brizo brushed nickel fixtures, and a Native Trails farmhouse sink.

Primary suite

The primary suite is a quiet escape filled with natural light. The en-suite bath features dual vanities, generous counter space, and a spacious walk-in shower, with a well-sized walk-in closet completing the retreat.

Elevated living

Upgraded luxury vinyl plank flooring runs throughout. The open great room connects the family and dining areas for easy everyday living and effortless entertaining, with upgraded baseboards, modern lighting, and a customized laundry room with added storage.

The backyard

The backyard is a blank canvas ready to become whatever suits your lifestyle, whether a cozy patio for entertaining, a fire pit lounge, a garden retreat, or a low-maintenance landscape.

Summary

Located within the highly regarded Riverside Unified School District, the home sits in one of Riverside's most desirable single-story communities. Residents enjoy HOA amenities including a playground, picnic and BBQ areas, and sport courts.
